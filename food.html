What's typical Mexican Cuisine?

Mexican food is very diverse with many option. Some noteable options include Pozole, Menudo , Tacos , and Tamales. Pozole is a soup made from pork meat and hominy and is usually eaten with cabbage or lettuce,radishes, lime and more. Menudo is also a popular soup dish in just like pozole but instead made with intestines.These are only a few of the many delicious foods Mexico has to offer. Listed below are the top 10 Mexican food according to Katja Gaskell in her article "Top 10 foods to try in Mexico".

While Mexicans have many delicious foods they also have delicious desserts aswell. Some Mexican desserts include Flan,Pan dulce(sweet bread), Tres Leche cake(three milk cake), and Arroz con leche(rice pudding) Pan dulce is commonly eaten with a side of coffee or hot choclate. Tres Leche cake is a cake commonly eaten at events such as parties, weddings, and more that is made with made with three different types of milk such as milk, condensed milk and evaported milk. Flan is a mexican custard dessert with Carmel on top.
